# Lorini

This is small application for learning English words used in courses.

### Features

- practice words for a chosen topic (meeting people, family etc.),
- check your knowledge for random words from all topics,
- reverse quizzes (you see the translation and need to recall the correct word),
- spaced repetition mode (words are reviewed at optimal intervals for long-term retention).
