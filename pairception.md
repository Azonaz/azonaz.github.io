# Pairception

**A calm number match puzzle.** Pair equal numbers or make 10 to clear the board.

Play a quick daily puzzle, continue a longer board, and keep your progress locally on your device.

## Match Numbers. Make 10. Clear The Board.

Pairception is a number-pair puzzle about finding clean connections.

Tap one number, then another. If the pair is valid, both numbers disappear from the board.

A pair can be removed when:

- the numbers are equal
- or their sum equals 10

The connection can run horizontally, vertically, diagonally, or across row boundaries when the path is clear.

The rules are simple enough to learn quickly. The board becomes more interesting when you start planning which pairs to remove now and which spaces to open for later.

## Small Details That Help

Pairception is designed for short sessions, but it gives you a few tools when the board gets tight.

### Adding Numbers

Use the Add button to extend the board.

It copies all currently active numbers and appends them to the grid in the same order. You can use Add up to 5 times per game.

### Hints

When you are stuck, a hint can show one valid pair.

It does not play the move for you. It only points to a pair you can choose to remove.

Each game starts with 3 hints. If you need more, you can get 3 additional hints for 5 shells.

### Row Clearing

When a full row becomes inactive, it disappears.

The remaining rows shift automatically, opening new connections and making the board easier to read.

## FAQ

### What is Pairception?

Pairception is a calm number match puzzle for iPhone and iPad. You clear the board by connecting equal numbers or pairs that add up to 10.

### How do valid pairs work?

Two numbers can be removed when they are equal or when their sum is 10. They also need a clear path between them: horizontally, vertically, diagonally, or across row boundaries in a continuous valid line.

### What does "make 10" mean?

It means two numbers can be paired when they add up to 10. For example, 1 and 9, 2 and 8, 3 and 7, or 4 and 6.

### Is there a daily puzzle?

Yes. Pairception has a new daily puzzle with a guaranteed solution. Daily puzzles do not use hints or adds.

### Does Pairception support Dark Mode?

Yes. Pairception supports light and dark appearance, following your device settings.

### Can I play offline?

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. Some features, such as ads, App Store purchases, or external services, may require a network connection.

### Does Pairception have ads?

Pairception uses ads and includes an optional purchase to remove them.

### Where is progress saved?

Progress and settings are saved locally on your device. Pairception does not require account registration.

### Is Pairception like Sudoku?

Pairception is not Sudoku and does not use Sudoku rules. It may appeal to players who enjoy quiet number logic, careful scanning, and planning a few moves ahead.

### Is Pairception like 2048?

Pairception is not 2048. It may appeal to players who like simple number rules with strategic depth, but the goal is different: you clear a board by matching equal numbers or making 10, rather than merging equal tiles into larger values.

### Is Pairception for iPhone or iPad?

Pairception is available on the App Store for iPhone and iPad.
